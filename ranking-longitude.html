A longitudinal study of UK universities

We’ve conducted a longitudinal study of UK universities by ranking them from West to East.

Consider this the UK’s Ivy League, in the sense that the top universities are geographically the closest to the Ivy League.

Longitudes are based on main postcodes only, with data from HESA.

Usual caveat applies to The Open University - yes, you could study with the OU in a boat in the middle of the Atlantic. But your postcode is Milton Keynes.

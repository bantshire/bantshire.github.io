The University Covid Car Park Rankings

The rankings of our times

We've compared universities' Covid cases to their car parking spaces, and ranked them accordingly. Genius, we know.

Covid case data is from the UCU Covid Dashboard*, and we've only used official data. And so, if anything, the universities in there rankings are all winners for being open and transparent about Covid on their campuses. Car parking data is from the lovely HESA**. All data is from March-ish 2021.

Health warning: Do not make any life-changing decisions based on this dodgy data. Table is for novelty purposes only. Stay safe.

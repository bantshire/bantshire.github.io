The Southern University Rankings

Bonjour! We've ranked UK universities on southernliness.

These rankings are very unique and in no way similar to the Northern University Rankings which we released in 2022.

Congratulations to the universites on the south coast for their strong performances, and better luck next time to Scotland's universities.

Latitudes are based on main postcodes only (date source: HESA).

Usual caveat applies to The Open University - yes, you could study on a Channel ferry. But your postcode is in Milton Keynes.

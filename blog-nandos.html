The Nando's University Rankings

We’ve ranked all UK universities based on their distance to the nearest Nando’s, and given them a Nando’s Excellence Framework (NEF) score of either Hot, Medium, or Mild.

Location data from the Unistats dataset (hesa.ac.uk) under the Creative Commons Attributation 4.0. Data returned regarding Nando’s distance relates to the information provided by nandos.co.uk. For the purposes of the NEF, all locations without a Nando’s are excluded - a list of these can be found in the 'Lemon and Herb' table.

Another pointless league table by The University of Bantshire, in collaboration with Data Ambassador @HoyleTime.

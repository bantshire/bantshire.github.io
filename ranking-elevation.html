The University Elevation Rankings

Working class people should aim ‘lower’ than Oxbridge, social mobility tsar Katharine Birbalsingh says.

But what does this mean?

Our data experts have been scratching their heads and assume this means lower in terms of height above sea level.

And so, introducing The University Elevation Rankings for 2022.

The average height of Oxbridge is 35m above sea level. Working class people should therefore consider the universities towards the bottom of this league table. We’re looking at you, Hull.

Height data is from getthedata.com/open-postcode-elevation. Postcode data is for main university postcodes only, from HESA.

Congratulations to Newman, Bath, and Keele on the dizzying height of your success.

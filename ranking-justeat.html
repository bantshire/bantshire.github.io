The Just Eat University Rankings

We’ve ranked all UK universities based on how many takeaways will deliver to their campuses (according to Just Eat), and given them a Gobble Excellence Framework (GEF, 2020) score of Stuffed, Peckish or Famished.

Location data from the Unistats dataset (hesa.ac.uk) under the Creative Commons Attributation 4.0. Data returned regarding number of takeaways who deliver to campuses relates to information provided by just-eat.co.uk.

All universities’ campuses are included, and are slightly weighted by the number of courses delivered (let's call it our "secret sauce").

Thanks again to our Senior Data Ambassador @HoyleTime who crunched the numbers - somebody please buy this man a takeaway. All data correct as October-ish 2020

If you would like to submit a complaint about the methodology, please print your letter of complaint and shred it immediately.
